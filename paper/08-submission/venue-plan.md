# Step 8 — Venue, Co-author, Timeline

Can be planned now; executed last.

## 8.1 Venue ladder (working paper first, then journal)
| Stage | Venue | Why | Gate |
|-------|-------|-----|------|
| 1 (first) | **arXiv / SSRN / NBER working paper** | citable immediately, no review gate, fits open posture | NBER needs an affiliated co-author |
| 2 | Data/methods journal — *Scientific Data*, *J. of Economic and Social Measurement* | rewards the pipeline + validated method | peer review |
| 2-alt | Applied econ / policy — *Brookings*, labor journal | rewards the labor-economics framing | peer review |
| 3 (if one clean result) | Short letter — PNAS-style brief | fastest, tightly scoped | high selectivity |

**Recommended:** post a working paper the moment Step 5 clears, then upgrade.
