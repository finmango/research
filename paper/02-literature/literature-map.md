## 2.1 The gap you own (draft — refine as you read)

Prior work measures **AI exposure** (which occupations are at risk) and,
recently, **realized employment effects** (Stanford Canaries; Anthropic
observed-exposure). Separately, a mature **search-nowcasting** literature
predicts unemployment from Google Trends. **No one has joined occupational
AI-exposure to household financial-stress signals at the state × occupation
level and validated it as a leading indicator.** That join is our contribution.
