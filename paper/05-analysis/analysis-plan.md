## 5.2 Robustness battery (reviewers WILL demand these)
- [ ] **Placebo (H2):** low-exposure occupations show no lead.
- [ ] **Exposure weighting sensitivity:** equal-weight vs. alternatives
      (promised in brief §3.1).
- [ ] **Search-saturation sensitivity (brief §8, #1 reviewer target):** show
      the result survives different baseline windows / detrending. Show raw
      vs. divergence-normalized side by side.
- [ ] **Augmentation vs. substitution (H3):** augmentation terms shouldn't
      predict; substitution terms should.
- [ ] **Out-of-sample:** does the signal beat a naive AR / no-signal baseline?
- [ ] **Suppression robustness:** results stable to the cell-size threshold.
